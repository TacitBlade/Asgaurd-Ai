# High-Value Digital Products: AI Automated Clipping for Beginner Streamers

The following digital products are designed specifically for beginner streamers who want to leverage AI for automated clipping and content repurposing. They provide deep value, actionable steps, and organized structures.

---

## 1. The Ultimate AI Clipping Setup Guide: From Zero to Viral Shorts

**Product Type:** In-Depth Implementation Guide / E-Book
**Target Buyer:** Beginner streamers who feel overwhelmed by editing and want a "set it and forget it" system to capture their best moments.
**Value Proposition:** Saves the user dozens of hours per week by teaching them exactly how to connect and configure AI clipping tools to automatically grab highlights without manual editing.

**High-Level Outline:**
*   **Module 1: The AI Clipping Landscape:** Understanding what AI clipping is, how it works (speech recognition, hype detection), and comparing top tools (e.g., Opus Clip, Eclipse, Cross Clip, Eklipse.gg).
*   **Module 2: Tool Selection & Budgeting:** A matrix for choosing the right tool based on the user's stream style (gaming vs. Just Chatting), budget (free vs. paid tiers), and desired output.
*   **Module 3: Step-by-Step Configuration:** Walkthroughs for connecting Twitch/YouTube to the chosen AI tool, setting up parameters (clip length, portrait framing), and handling VOD imports.
*   **Module 4: Prompting the AI:** How to train the AI (if applicable) or use specific keywords/markers during the live stream to ensure the AI catches the exact moments you want.
*   **Module 5: The Workflow Loop:** A daily schedule showing how to export, quickly review, and queue the AI-generated clips for social media without spending more than 15 minutes a day.

## 4. The 30-Day "Clip to Grow" Training Pack

**Product Type:** 4-Week Action Plan & Video Mini-Course
**Target Buyer:** Streamers who need a structured, day-by-day roadmap to build the habit of streaming, capturing AI clips, and posting consistently.
**Value Proposition:** Takes the user by the hand for their first month, providing daily bite-sized tasks that guarantee they will have an automated clipping system running and 30 pieces of short-form content published by the end.

**High-Level Outline:**
*   **Week 1: System Building:** (Days 1-7) Setting up the stream, connecting the AI tool, running the first test stream, and reviewing the first batch of automated clips.
*   **Week 2: The Editing Polish:** (Days 8-14) Learning the basic "polish" (adding auto-captions, adjusting crop, applying the Toolkit assets). Goal: Edit 5 AI clips.
*   **Week 3: Distribution & Scheduling:** (Days 15-21) Setting up free scheduling tools (e.g., Metricool, Buffer), batch-scheduling the edited clips, and writing SEO-friendly titles/descriptions.
*   **Week 4: Review & Refine:** (Days 22-30) Analyzing the data from the first posted clips. Adjusting AI clipping settings based on what the audience actually engaged with, and setting up the long-term sustainable workflow. Included: A "Sprint Review" worksheet.
